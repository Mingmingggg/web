HISTORY

Established in 2018, Infobytes Solutions Inc. (ISI) is dedicated to providing advanced and innovative information technology products and services. Recognizing the opportunity to serve as a System Integrator for State Universities, ISI has positioned itself as a key player in the sector.

INDUSTRY EXPERIENCE

For the past seven years, ISI has been honored to partner with some of the Philippines' most esteemed State Universities, enhancing their digital educational platforms. Our clientele includes Mindanao State University - General Santos, Mindanao State University at Naawan, Mindanao State University - Main Campus Marawi, Mindanao State University - Sulu, and Mindanao State University - Tawi-Tawi College of Technology and Oceanography.

PRODUCT RANGE

ISI engages in the retail and wholesale of computer components while delivering comprehensive information technology solutions. These solutions encompass data storage, servers, software licenses, security measures, and the trading of related merchandise.
